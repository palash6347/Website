Education

University of Kansas Medical Center

First year PhD student • August 2017-present

Currently working as a research assistant in Biostatistics department and also associated with KU Alzheimer's disease center. My main goal is to develop R Shiny Apps to visualize the data more interactively.

University of Nevada,Reno

Master Degree in Statistics• summer 2017

My master’s thesis is Generalized family of Univariate distributions and a new asymmetric laplace model under the supervision of Dr.Tomasz J. Kozubowski. In this thesis, we aimed to develop a novel family of distributions which belongs to the exponential family. Our goal is to develop new inference procedure and perform simulation to assess the procedure. I have been doing a project on Time Series Analysis using CDC(Center for disease control) data. I also do a project related to Count data Analysis using zero inflated Poisson Regression model.I am using Random effect model to analyze my count data. I perform several tests like Pasaran CD test whether the residuals are correlated across entities.

University of Dhaka, Bangladesh

Bachelor Degree in Mathematics• Dec. 2014

My capstone project involves Dynamics of a multi stage Infectious disease model with Erlang distributed delay. I investigated the dynamics of infection disease by considering a multistage infection model called S I k R model. Using this special kind of Erlang distributed delay model, I concluded that the final size of an epidemic outbreak not only depends on disease characteristics, but also on the number of disease stages.
